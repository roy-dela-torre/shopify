Overview

Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate the women in your life. Flasked has curated a list of thoughtful drink gifts for her that emphasize diversity and suit many preferences.

Champagne and Sparkling Wine

There's something romantic about a glass of champagne and sparkling wine. This goes perfectly with a romantic candlelit dinner, a cozy movie night, or a lively celebration — whatever set-up you have prepared, these drinks can go perfectly with it.

This can be the perfect gift for your significant other if they’re celebrating big milestones in their life. Were they recently promoted at work? Have they been on a consistent streak in their productivity routine? Every moment is worth celebrating with a bottle of Martini Asti Spumante Sparkling Wine. The refreshing crispiness of this drink awakens her taste buds with each sip.

Premium Wine

Is your girlfriend a wine collector? If yes, then take their collection up a notch with a bottle of premium wine. There are a wide variety of bottles that we offer this service for including the Marques de Riscal Gran Reserva Red Wine and the Santa Carolina Herencia Red Wine.

For Wine Collectors

A premium wine adds a unique and rare gem to her collection, especially if it's from a renowned vineyard or a limited edition. This will certainly be a meaningful addition to their curated assortment. They know these wines to offer complex flavors and tasting notes, providing an elevated and memorable tasting experience for enthusiasts.

For Casual Drinkers

For those who enjoy the occasional glass of wine, a premium bottle because a special treat. And what better event than Valentine’s Day? This gift shows that you've put thought into selecting something beyond the ordinary.

Local Gin

If your girlfriend appreciates the art of mixology, a different kind of gin for her collection might be the perfect gift. You can explore some of the products that our country offers like the Agimat Gin from Destileria Barako. There is a flavor of spices, citrus, and floral notes unique to the Philippines including Ylang Ylang, dalandan, and Libas fruit.

This not only supports local craftsmanship but also offers a taste of the Philippines' unique botanicals and flavors. From calamansi and sampaguita to indigenous herbs, local gins showcase the country's rich biodiversity. This thoughtful gift adds a touch of Filipino identity to your celebration, creating a shared experience rooted in the culture. It's more than a bottle of gin; it's a celebration of your love and the vibrant spirit of the Philippines in every sip.

Specialty Liqueur

Add a bit of flavor to her life with some specialty liquor for her cocktails. These drinks add a burst of personality whether it’s the velvety richness of chocolate or vibrant fruit flavors. This is the perfect gift for girlfriends who enjoy hosting girls’ nights in their place.

These drinks add that extra layer of complexity to a drink, introducing unique flavors and aromas that tantalize the taste buds. For your coffee lovers, they’ll appreciate the Walsh coffee liqueur. This cocoa flavor is a versatile ingredient that is used even for your cocktails. It adds a different level of depth. Think Espresso Martini!

Aged Rum

To the women in your life who love sophisticated and rich flavors in their drinks, aged rum is the perfect gift for them! These liquours like the Bacardi Aged Collection Pack have profiles that cater to their refined taste buds. This is not everyone’s cup of team, but to the women in your life who enjoy it, why not consider it for Valentine’s?
