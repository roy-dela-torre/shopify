About Us

Flasked started as a passion project that aimed to give an affordable option for people that want to try different types of whisky. Our operations started in late 2019 in a living room that had a couple of bottles of whisky on top of a coffee table. After facing setbacks in our first couple of months, we decided to get into merchandising to help sustain the business. Each month we grew adding more products. Now it has evolved to a full retail business with more than a thousand products of all types of alcoholic beverages.

Since 2019

Flasked has now built relationships with numerous local and international brands and is looking to find more to give the customers a better experience. The introduction of gifting sets and customization would give an avenue for the brand to widen their reach and the customer to add personalization to their bottle. Building greater customer relationships will be our next focus to increase interaction with the end users.
