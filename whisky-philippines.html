What is Whisky?

Also known as the "water of life," whisky is a delightful alcoholic drink created through the distillation of fermented grain mash. This refined beverage gains rich and captivating flavors from the careful aging process in wooden barrels, and each type offers a distinctive taste and aroma tailored to suit different palates, from Scotch to bourbon and Japanese whisky.
